Front End Dev & UX Designer

Hi! I'm a passionate Front-End Web Developer and an UX Designer, with a background as a Volunteer Firefighter! My designs are focused to people who cares about their users and wants to make their products feel accessible for all of them!

Since I was a little kid, I always tried my best to make everyone feel accepted, and that's the main goal with my work. I understand this objective is as essential as every other component for a product/company to grow, and if you keep scrolling, you are probably aware of this too.

Professional Approaches

The things that get my full attention and which I value the most doing my work

Accessibility

Could you imagine not being able to get certain services or visualizing a page only because you have a limitation that you can't control? Yeah, me neither

User Psychology

Why does the user take that direction? How does the Next Billion Users mindset change through time? What does user bias affect? Understanding the way of thinking of your user/client makes the difference between you and your competitor

Attention to detail

The position of a button, the Von Restorff Effect and the least amount of distance the cursor needs to take. It's like what its said: "You never notice a good design, only a bad one"
